Product Overview

The WEP02 is a curbside smart parking terminal that combines ultrasonic vehicle detection with an integrated LPR camera system. Deployed on existing street poles with zero ground infrastructure, it delivers real-time occupancy data, automated plate recognition, and violation evidence — all in a single weatherproof unit.

Dual Detection Mode

Combines ultrasonic sonar with camera-based computer vision. Cross-validates occupancy status to eliminate false positives even in rain, fog, or direct sunlight.

LPR — Plate Recognition

High-definition licence plate recognition at entry and exit. Automated session matching and violation evidence capture with timestamped HD photos.

Revenue Recovery

Verifies every session against payment records in real time. Flags non-paying vehicles instantly, enabling enforcement response before the driver returns.

Cloud-Native Integration

Push data over 4G/5G/LoRa to the Trivium City Command Platform or any third-party PMS via open REST API. No proprietary lock-in.

All-Weather Resilience

IP67-rated enclosure, operating range −30°C to +70°C. Pole-mounted design handles vibration, wind load, and direct water exposure without performance degradation.

Flexible Power

Powered via standard pole supply or optional solar panel add-on. Built-in battery backup maintains operation during grid outages up to 48 hours.
