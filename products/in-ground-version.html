Product Overview

The WEP03 is a low-position smart parking sensor combining a wide-angle camera with ultrasonic/radar dual detection. Equipped with a 1/2.8" CMOS 2MP sensor, 4G/5G full-network connectivity, IP68 protection, and a solar-charged lithium battery lasting up to 180 days. Captures license plate, colour, images, date and time in real time — with full offline operation support.

Wide-Angle Camera + Dual Detection

1/2.8-inch CMOS sensor with ≥2MP resolution. Wide-angle camera combined with ultrasonic/radar for cross-validated detection. LPR accuracy ≥97% by day and ≥94% by night. Collects plate number, colour, vehicle images, date and time.

4G/5G Full-Network Transmission

Supports 4G/5G full-network communication via TCP/IP. Vehicle data, images and session records are automatically uploaded to the platform in real time. After network recovery, offline data is auto-synced seamlessly.

Real-Time Data Collection

Detects vehicle information in real time: license plate number, plate colour, vehicle images, collection date and time. Stores data with integrity and security — storage capacity ≥8GB, retention ≥10 days.

Offline Operation + Auto-Recovery

Fully operational without network connectivity. Data is cached locally and automatically uploaded/downloaded once the network is restored. One-key automatic recovery function ensures continuity even after system faults.

Fault Self-Diagnosis + Remote Upgrade

Built-in fault self-diagnosis, anti-interference protection, and remote software module control. Firmware upgrades are performed remotely — no on-site visits required. Automatic time synchronisation and calibration included.

Solar-Ready Battery — 180 Days

Built-in lithium battery with solar charging support. Comprehensive battery life ≥180 days — industry-leading endurance for zero-maintenance deployment. IP68-rated body withstands submersion, dust and vehicle impact from −20°C to +60°C.
