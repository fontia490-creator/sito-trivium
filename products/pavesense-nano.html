Product Overview

The PaveSense Nano is an ultra-low-profile in-ground parking sensor that combines magnetic field detection with ultrasonic ranging for class-leading accuracy. Installed flush with the pavement surface, it is invisible to drivers and impervious to weather, vandalism, and vehicle impact. No trenching, no power cabling — simply drill, drop, and connect.

HD Camera + LPR >99%

2MP sensor with 120° field of view. Automatically switches day/night modes, supports low-light capture, and uses ultrasonic feedback triggering for precise shot timing. License plate recognition accuracy exceeds 99%.

4G Real-Time Transmission

Vehicle entry/exit events, images, and session data are automatically uploaded over 4G to the Trivium City Command Platform in real time. Device status is monitored and reported continuously.

Smart Alarm System

Automatically detects and alerts for overstay violations, non-motorised vehicle occupancy (bicycles, tricycles), and low battery conditions — enabling proactive enforcement and maintenance.

15-Minute Installation

No road breaking, no wiring, no cables. The WEP01 is installed directly on the road surface without disrupting traffic or requiring civil works — fully operational in just 15 minutes per space.

Full Metal IP67 Body

Streamlined full-metal enclosure rated IP67 for dust and waterproofing, plus impact resistance. Designed to prevent tire scraping and blowout even under direct vehicle contact. Operates from −20°C to +70°C.

Solar-Ready Battery

Equipped with 18650 lithium battery pack supporting both standard charging and optional solar panel integration. Remote management allows firmware upgrades and factory resets without on-site visits.
