How Trivium Works

A fully integrated stack — from ground-level sensors to city-wide cloud intelligence — delivering sub-2-second data latency across thousands of spaces.

Ground Sensors

Magnetic + LiDAR dual-detection. IP68 rated, 5-year battery. Sub-second occupancy updates via LoRa or NB-IoT.

Edge Processing

Local compute for offline resilience. Data is buffered and synced. Solar-powered with IP67 enclosure.

Cloud Intelligence

Multi-tenant SaaS platform with open REST APIs. Integrates with ERP, MaaS, and municipal systems.

Predictive AI
